About Me

I make audio synthesis and effect plugins, new digital instruments, and various software projects mostly relating to audio and music. Currently, I'm attending McGill University in Montreal pursuing a Master's degree in Music Technology at the Input Devices and Music Interaction Laboratory.

Projects

Slapbox

A standalone digital percussion instrument built using the Bela platform. It captures both excitation (strikes) and modulation (continuous pressure) gestures to allow exploration of new percussive interactions. The top, side and back panels all act as interaction surfaces.

View Project

Mapper4Live

A software plugin made for the digital audio workstation software Ableton Live. Mapper4Live exposes Ableton’s synthesis and effect parameters on the distributed libmapper signal mapping network, providing new opportunities for interaction between software and hardware synths, audio effects, and controllers. This enables libmapper-compatible devices such as Probatio and the T-Stick to control Ableton synthesis and effect parameters with their gestural signals.

Audioform

Rotating cylindrical spectrum visualizer built in Unity3D that reacts to whatever music is being played on the device (Windows only). Audioform builds a continuous 3D mesh of the music's spectrum, taking the viewer through a sonic reactive wormhole.

Harmonigon v2

An interactive harmonic table allowing the user to play on a piano-like interface rearranged for chord creation. Includes automated harmonic path creation for playing chords or patterns of notes.

Earsdrop

Earsdrop is an Android app that allows users to tune in to strangers’ music in real time. It requires that both users have the application installed, and have Spotify Premium accounts. One user is the broadcaster, sending out their music live to users around them, and the other is the listener, tuning in to the broadcaster’s music. It works in the background for the broadcaster, all they need to do is play music through Spotify as normal. The listener simply chooses which user around them they would like to listen to (within a half mile radius), and get to listen to the broadcaster’s current track at the same position as the broadcaster.
